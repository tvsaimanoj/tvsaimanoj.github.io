I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Missouri University of Science & Technology. My advisor is Prof. Tony Luo.

Previously, I received my Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Arlington. Prior to that, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Indian Institute of Science, where I was advised by Prof. Y Narahari. I also worked as an intern at Dassault Systems and SCSK Corporation. I obtained my Bachelors degree from MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology, India.

Research Interests

The current focus of my research is at the nexus of Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning, Cyber Security & Privacy. I'm specifically working on:

  • Developing Robust Learning Algorithms against Adversarial Attacks
  • Safeguarding IoT Systems against Security & Privacy Risks
  • Enabling Communication-Efficient Edge Intelligence in IoT

Previously, I worked on Social Network Analytics, Bitcoin, Blockchain & Decentralized Applications (DApps).
