About Me

I'm currently a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Missouri S&T. My advisors are Prof. Tony Luo and Prof. Sid Nadendla.

Prior to S&T, I completed my Master's in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Arlington. I worked as a Research Assistant at the Indian Institute of Science under Prof. Y Narahari and as an intern at Dassault Systèmes. I obtained a B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering from MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology, India.

I am a Student Member of ACM & IEEE.

Research Interests

My areas of interest are at the nexus of Machine Learning, Cybersecurity and Game Theory. Specifically, I work on Adversarial Attacks in Federated Learning.
